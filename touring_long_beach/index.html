About Long Beach

Long Beach is a fun loving city to visit. This city is has a diverse culture that brings many different walks of life. Long Beach has things to do for everyone, such as visiting the beach, going to the museums, art walks, farmers markets, flea markets, Clubs, sporting events, conventions and stay at great hotels.

Population

The estimated population for Long Beach is about 473,577 people as of 2014.
