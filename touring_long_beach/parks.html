Bluff Park

Bluff Park is located between Junipero Ave and Loma Ave. This park stretches along side ocean blvd which allows for a great scenic view. At bluff park you can enjoy the view of the ocean and view of the ports and Shorelne Village. This park is open 24 hours a day which allow access to beach. You can find an assortment of crowd jogging, walking and enjoying the view.

Colorado Beach Lagoon Park

Colorado Beach Lagoon Park has some unique features because its located by some wetlands that some sandy beaches fo swimming. This park has some picnic areas to enjoy family gatherings, and great natural views. .

Deforest Park

DeForest park is a hidden neigborhood park that rest along side the long riverbed. At Deforest Park there is small nature trail that is open to the public. You can enjoy some sports games here on saturday depending on the season and occasional park movies when they are scheduled. You can see many people working out here becuase of the access to the riverbed which spans about 70 miles and leads all the way to the Long Beach Coast.

El Dorado Park

El Dorado park is huge park that consists of three sections. The tthree sections are the El Dorado East Regional Park, El Dorado Park West and El Dorado Nature Center. Here you can do an assortment of activites such as archery, Fishing, bike trails, over night camping, nature center, and us of picninc areas.
