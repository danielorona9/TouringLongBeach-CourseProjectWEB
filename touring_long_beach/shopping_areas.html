The Pike

The Pike is a shopping center that is located closed to the beach. This shopping cener includes a Cinemark theatre, Nike outlet, Forever 21 Red, H&M, and other retail stores. For entertainment there are restaurants such as Famous Dave's, Bubba Gump Shrimp Co., etc. Also The Pike is closely located to the Aquariam of the Pacific, Shoreline Village and downtown Pine Ave.

Location: 95 S. Pine Ave. Long Beach, CA. 90802 Long Beach, CA 90802

Shoreline Village

Shoreline village is great place to visit. This area is full of shops that offer unique items. One of my favorite shops here actually sells a whole variety of different hot sauces. There also deliscous deserts like funnel cakes that can be bought here to satify your sweet tooth. The Shoreline Village is also walking distance fom The Pike, Shoreline Walk and the Shoreline Park for a beautiful view.

Location: 401-435 Shoreline Village Drive, Long Beach, CA. 90802
