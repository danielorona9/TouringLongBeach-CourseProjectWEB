Beaches

The Beach coatstline in Long Beach Streches 4 mile across. The Coast of Long Beach has many beaches such as Alamitos Beach, Junipero Beach, Belmont Shore Beach, Rosie's Beach, Granada Beach and Pensinsula Beach.

What can you do at the Beach

The beaches at Long Beach are full of activities. Some activites include Biking, Roller blading, Surfing, fishing and the pier, hanging out at the shoreline Village and at the lighthouse. The Junipero Beach is also one of few beaches thats never closed to the public. You can find people late night hanging out on the beach walking dogs, running or just sitting out on the sand. One fun event that happens is Gruneon which is when little fish come to the shoreline to breed during full moons
